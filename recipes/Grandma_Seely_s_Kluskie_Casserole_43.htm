A simple casserole that can't be beat!
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1 package Kluskie noodles large bag
1 pound Ground beef
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1 Onion large
1 can Cream of mushroom soup
1 can Corn
1 package Sour cream 8 ounces or more
 
Instructions
 Brown hamburger and onions together in skillet. Drain greese. Cook noodles in a pot while browning the hamburger. Add noodles to the skillet with the browned hamburger. Add cans of cream of chicken and cream of mushroom. Add can of corn. Finally, add the sour cream to the skillet. Then pour mixture into 9x13 casserole dish and cook 30 minutes at 350°.
