Grandma Dastrups Special Oatmeal

Directions

For one single serving.....

1 cup boiling water, add 1/2 cup old fashioned oatmeal bring to rolling boil, cover and lower heat to low.

Cook about 5 mins, when water is evaporated and large oatmeal flakes are visible.

Turn off heat and add 1 Tablespoon butter, 1 Tablespoon powdered milk and 1 Tablespoon brown sugar.

Cover and let sit until butter is melted, stir together and Yum!
