Yields: 8 Servings

Ingredients

8 pieces Chicken
1/3 cup Flour
1/2 teaspoon Paprika
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1 dash Pepper
1 dash Thyme
5 tablespoons Butter
1/4 cup Onion chopped
1 cup Chicken soup stock*
1/2 bunch Light cream
1/4 teaspoon Lemon Juice
2 tablespoons Pimiento


 
Instructions
 Dredge chicken pieces with mixture of flour, paprika, salt, pepper and thyme. Brush chicken with 3 tablespoons melted butter and brown in oven, or shake off excess flour and saute in about 3 tablespoons of cooking oil.
Saute onion lightly in 2 tablespoons butter for 5 minutes. Stir in excess flour from dredgeing chicken. Add soup stock and thick. Add lemon juice, then pimiento; blend well. Pour sauce over chicken in a 2- to 3-quart casserole; cover and bake 1¼ hours at 325°F., or until chicken is fork tender. serve over cooked rice.

* Or use 2 chicken bouillon cubes, or 1½ teaspoons chicken soup base granules and 1 cup water
