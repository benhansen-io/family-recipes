everyone's favorite breakfast casserole
 Yields: 8 Servings

Ingredients

5 Eggs
1 stick Butter
2 cups Milk
8 slices Bread cubed
8 ounce Sharp cheese grated
1 can Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup Milk
1 pound Sausage


 
Instructions
 arrange cubes in 9 X 13 baking dish. Sprinkle 8 onz cheese over bread. beat 5 eggs into melted butter. add 2 cups milk- pour this mixture over bread and cheese cover tightly and refrigerate overnight.
Before baking mix 1 can cream of mushroom soup undiluted with 1/2 cup milk. spread over top. bake at 350 for 1 hour.
1 pound sausage should be fried, drained, and added to casserole prior to overnight refrigeration
