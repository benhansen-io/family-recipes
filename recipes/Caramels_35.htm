World's best homemade caramels- from Rosemary Williams, through Grandma jean Hansen
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

2 cups Corn syrup Karo brand
2 cups White sugar
1 cup Whipping cream
1 square Butter cut into 5-6 sections
1/2 can Evaporated milk (1/2 cup plus 1/3 cup)
1 cup Pecans chopped
1 teaspoon Vanilla


 
Instructions
 mix corn syrup and sugar (off heat) stir well. Put on heat, bring to a full rolling boi and medium (medium to high) heatl. Stir occasionally while it comes to a boil. After it starts boiling add alternately using the three additions: whipping cream, canned milk, and butter
Stir constantly while boiling. Stir until temperature reaches 228 degrees. Remove from heat. Add pecans if desired. great either way. Add vanilla (manatory) beat for 1 minute then pour into greased 9x13 pan. Cool in refrigerator.
