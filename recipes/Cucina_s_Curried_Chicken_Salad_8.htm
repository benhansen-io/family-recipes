s Cucina's Curried Chicken Salad s   


Note: Since we stared serving this salad, not a day goes by when we don't sell out. It is easy, flavorfull and with the addition of the apricot perserves, it gives the salad a sweet tangy edge to the chicken. The perfect lucheon salad, over greens, or just in a thick roll for a great sandwich.
 Yields: 8 Servings

Ingredients

Salad:
2 pounds Boneless, skinless cold poached chicken breasts cu
4 ribs Celery thinly sliced on diagonal
2 cups Red grapes
1/2 cup Walnut chopped
1/2 cup Golden raisins
Dressing:
1 cup Mayonnaise
1/4 cup Half-and-half cream
1/2 cup Apricot perserves
1/4 cup Rice wine vinegar
2 tablespoon Curry powder
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon White pepper


 
Instructions
 Salad:
In a large mixing bowl, combine the poached chicken, sliced celery, red grapes, walnuts and raisins.
Dressing:
In a small bowl, whisk all ingredients for dressing. Gently fold the dressing into the salad. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
