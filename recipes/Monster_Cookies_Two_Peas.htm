Monster Cookies (Two Peas)

Source: Two peas and Their Pod recipe book

contributor: JEH 3.10.24

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups old fashioned oats
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temp.
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup chocolate chips
  • ¾ cup M&M candies
  • Flake sea salt, for sprinkling on cookies

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350° and line two large baking sheets with baking mats or parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, stirred together the oats, flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Using a stand mixer or handheld mixer, beat the butter, peanut butter, and sugars together until creamy and smooth, on medium speed. Scrape down the sides of the bowl with a spatula, if necessary. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  4. Add in the dry ingredients and mix on low until just until they combine. don't over mix. Gently stir in the chocolate chips and M&Ms.
  5. Scoop the cookies into round balls, about 2 tablespoons of dough, per cookie, and place on the prepared cookie sheets, leaving 2 inches in between cookies. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the cookies are lightly browned around the edges, but still soft in the middle.
  6. Remove cookies from oven and sprinkle the cookies with sea salt. You can also gently press additional chocolate chips and on top of the cookies to make them look extra pretty. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for about five minutes or until set. When set, transfer cookies to a wire rack and cool completely.
