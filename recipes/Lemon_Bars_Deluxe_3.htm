Yields: 30 Pieces

Ingredients

2 cups Flour sifted
1/2 cup Confectioners Sugar sifted
1 cup Butter
4 Egg beaten
2 cups Granulated sugar
1/3 cup Lemon Juice
1/4 cup Flour
1/2 teaspoon Baking powder


 
Instructions
 Sift together the 2 cups flour and the confectioners sugar. Cut in butter or margerine until mixture clings together. Press in 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking pan. Bake in 350째 oven for 20-25 minutes or until lightly browned. Beat together eggs, granulated sugar and lemon juice. Sift together the 1/4 cup flour and the baking powder; stir into egg mixture. Pour over baked cruse. Bake 350째 for 25 minutes longer, sprinkle with additional confectioners sugar. Cool. Cut in bars.
