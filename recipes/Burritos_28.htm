Ingredients

1 pound Ground beef
1 Onion small and diced
1 teaspoon Coriander ground
1 teaspoon Cumin
1 can Beans refried
1 cup Cheddar cheese grated
1 package Sour cream
1 package Guacamola
1 package Flour tortillas


 
Instructions
 Brown hamburger with onion, season with salt and pepper. Add the following seasonings: coriander, cumin. Add 1 can refried beans. May add salsa to mixture or just add when serving. Put mixture in flour tortillas and top with cheese. Roll up and microwave until cheese melts. Serve with salsa, sour cream and guacolmola.
