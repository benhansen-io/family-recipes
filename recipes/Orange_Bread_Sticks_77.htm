Yields: 8 Sticks

Ingredients

1 package Dry yeast or 1 yeast cake
1/4 cup Warm water
1 cup Milk
1/2 cup Shortening or butter
1/3 cup Sugar
1 teaspoon Salt
1 Egg
5 cups Flour sifted
1/4 cup Orange juice
2 tablespoon Orange peel grated
Frosting:
1 teaspoon Grated orange peel
2 tablespoon Orange juice
1 cup Powdered sugar


 
Instructions
 Dissolve yeast in warm water. Scald milk and add shortening, sugar and salt. Bear 2 eggs well and add to cooled milk mixture. Add yeast and 2 cups flour. Stir well. Add orange juice and rind and remaining flour to make a soft dough. Cover and let rest for 10 minutes. Knead 5 to 6 minutes. Place in greased bowl. Cover and let rise until doubled (about 2 hours). Divide dough into 8 well greased bread pans. Cover and let rise until double. Bake at 375 for 12 minutes. Cool and frost with orange frosting made by blending grated rind, orange juice, and powdered sugar.
