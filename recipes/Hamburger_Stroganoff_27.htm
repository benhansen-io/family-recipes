Yields: 4 Servings

Ingredients

1/2 cup Onions minced
1 clove Garlic minced
2 tablespoons Butter
1 pound Ground beef
2 tablespoons White wheat flour
1 teaspoon Salt
dash Pepper
4 ounces Mushrooms sliced, including liquid
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup undiluted
1 cup Sour cream commercial
2 tablespoons Parsley minced
 
Instructions
 Saute onion and garlic in butter over medium heat. Add meat and brown. Add flour, salt, pepper and mushrooms (including juice). Cook 5 minutes. Add soup and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes. Just before serving stir in sour cream. Heat through. Sprinkle with parsley. Serve over hot noodles.
