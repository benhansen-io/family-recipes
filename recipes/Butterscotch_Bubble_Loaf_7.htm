Yields: 1 cake

Ingredients

24 Rhodes Dinner Rolls thawed but still cold
1/2 box Butterscotch or vanilla pudding mix non instant
1/2 cup Peacans chopped
1/2 cup Brown sugar
1/2 cup Margarine


 
Instructions
 Cut dinner rolls in half and dip in dry pudding mix. Arrange rolls in sprayed bundt pan, alternately with pecans. Sprinkle any remaining pudding mix over the top. Combine brown sugar and butter. Heat together until butter is melted and syrup is formed (microwave about 1 1/2 minutes). Pour syrup over rolls. Cover with sprayed plastic wrap. Let riser until double in size or even with top of bundt pan. Carefully remove wrap. Bake at 350°F 30-35 minutes. Cover with foil the last 15 minutes of baking. Immediately after baking, loosen from sides of the pan with a knife and invert onto a serving pan.
