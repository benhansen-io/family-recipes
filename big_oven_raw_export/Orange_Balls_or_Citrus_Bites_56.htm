Ingredients

1 package (12 oz) Vanilla wafers
1 cup Pecan pieces
1 package (16oz) Powdered sugar
6 ounces Frozen orange juice concentrate thawed
1/2 cup Butter melted
2 cup Coconut


 
Instructions
 Process vanilla waffers in food processor until ground. Process pecans in processor until ground. Combine all ingrediants except coconuts. (by hand). Roll into 1" balls. Roll in coconut. Freeze well.
