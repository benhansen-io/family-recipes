A tasty dish from Lisa Sandstrom
 Yields: 4 Servings

Ingredients

2/3 produce bag Roma tomatoes chopped
1/2 Red onion chopped
1 Anaheim Pepper minced (take seeds out first)
2 Serano Peppers (take seeds out) minced
2 cloves Garlic minced
1 bunch Cilantro chopped
1 1/2 teaspoons Sea Salt
1 1/2 teaspoons Chill pepper
1 1/2 Fresh lime (juice)
1 teaspoon Cumin


 
Instructions
 Taste. If too spicy add more tomato. (Lisa made it for us without any cumin and it tasted great). Lisa says she adds different things everytime she makes it.
