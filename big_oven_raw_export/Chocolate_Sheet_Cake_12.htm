a popular recipe from Jane Davis
 Yields: 4 Servings

Ingredients

2 cups White wheat flour
2 sugar
1 1/2 cups Baking soda
1/2 teaspoon Salt
2 squares Margarine
4 tablespoon Cocoa powder
1 cup Water
3/4 cup Buttermilk
2 Eggs
1 teaspoon Vanilla
3/4 squares Margarine
1 1/2 tablespoon Cocoa powder
4 1/2 tablespoon Buttermilk
1 teaspoon Vanilla to taste
 
Instructions
 mix together: 2 C flour, 2 cups sugar, baking soda and salt. Take 2 squares margarine,, 4 T cocoa, and water. Bring to a boil. Pour liquid over dry ingredients. Add 3/4 c buttermilk, 2 beaten eggs and 1 teaspoon vanilla (mix will be very soupy)
Bake in greased and floured jelly roll pan. Bake 20 minutesat 350.
ICING:
bring to boil:
3/4 square margarine
1 1/2 T cocoa
4 1/2 T buttermilk
remove from stove and add powdered sugar until desired consistency. Also add 1 teaspoon vanilla
