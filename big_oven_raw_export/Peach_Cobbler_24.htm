s Peach Cobbler s   



Yields: 0


Ingredients

3 cups Sliced Peaches drained (or 2 large cans (29oz))
2/3 cup Sugar
2 tablespoons Flour
1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon
2 tablespoons Butter or Margarine
1 cup Flour
2 tablespoons Sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons Baking powder
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/3 cup Salad Oil
3 tablespoons Milk
1 Egg


 
Instructions
 Place peaches in bottom of 9x9 pan (or double recipe and place in a 9x13 pan).
Mix first set of sugar, flour and cinnamon (ingredients 2-4) and sprinkle over peaches. Then cut up butter and dot over mixture.
Mix second set of flour, sugar, baking powder and salt (ingredients 6-9) and set aside. In another bowl mix salad oil, milk, and the egg. Combine two mixtures together and spoon thi batter mixture over and dot around pan.

Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.

Serve with cream or ice cream.

For a 9x13 pan double recipe.
