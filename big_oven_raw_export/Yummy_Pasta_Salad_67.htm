Ingredients

1 package (1 lb) White spiral pasta (don't use all)
1 package (1 lb) Bow tie pasta
6 Chicken breasts Boiled for 20 minutes (covered so they don't dry out) and then chopped
2 cans (8 oz) Pineapple tidbits drained
2 cans Sliced water chestnuts
2 cups Celery diced fine
1/2 package Craisen
4 Green Onions sliced
1 big can or 2 small cans Mandarin Oranges
1 Gala apple chopped
lots Red grapes sliced
1 big bottle or 2 smal Kraft coleslaw dressing
1 cup Mayonnaise


 
Instructions
 Combine first 11 ingredients in very large bowl. Then pour the Kraft coleslaw dressing and mayo over.
