Ingredients

1 1/2 cups Butter flavored crisco
2 1/2 cups Brown sugar packed
2 cups Sugar
6 Eggs
3 cups Crunchy Peanut butter
2 tablespoons Light Corn Syrup
4 teaspoons Baking Soda
2 teaspoons Vanilla
9 cups Quick Oats
Chocolate Chips
White Chocolate Chips
M&Ms
Reeses Pieces
Butterscotch Chips


 
Instructions
 Combine shortening and sugar until blended. Add next 5 ingredients. Stir in oats and any goodies you want. Using ice creme scoop (should be 1/4 cup) drop cookies 3' apart on ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten out dough and bake in a 350 degreee oven for 11 minutes. Cool in pan for about 8 minutes then move to wire rack.
