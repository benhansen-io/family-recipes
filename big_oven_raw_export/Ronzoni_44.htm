Everone's favorite dish
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1 package Ronzoni jumbo shells
1 pound Ground beef browned with the garlic cloves
6 cloves Garlic
1 pound Monterey jack cheese cubed
4 slices Bread crust removed, cubed
1 Egg
1/2 cup Milk
2 tablespoons Fresh parsley
1 jar Prego Sauce


 
Instructions
 Mix altogether into shells;
Pour over shells with prego; cover with foil, bake at 350°F until bubbling
