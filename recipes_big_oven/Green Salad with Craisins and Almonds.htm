s Green Salad with Craisins and Almonds s   



Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1 Red Onion
2 cans Mandarin Oranges
1 bag Craisins
2 head Lettuces (one iceberg and one red leaf)
1 Jicama (long strips)
1 cup Almonds sugared
6 tablespoons Granulated white sugar
Dressing:
2 teaspoons Salt
dash Pepper
1/2 cup Oil
2 tablespoons Parsley chopped
4 tablespoons Sugar
4 tablespoons Red wine vinegar
dash Tabasco sauce


 
Instructions
 Combine 1cup alomnds and 6 tablespoon sugar in a small saucepan and stir on medium heat. The sugar will start to liquify and turn brownish. When it starts to attach itself to the almonds, remove from heat.
Dressing (can blend in advance and refigerate):
Blend salt, pepper, oil, chopped parsley, sugar, red wine vinegar, and tabasco sauce.
