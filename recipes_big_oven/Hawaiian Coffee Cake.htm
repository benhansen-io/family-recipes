Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1 1/2 cup Flour
2/3 cup Sugar
2 1/2 teaspoons Baking Powder
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1 can (8 oz) Crushed Pineapple drained
1 Egg beaten
3/4 cup Coconut
1/2 cup Butter melted
1/2 cup Brown Sugar


 
Instructions
 Stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Add pineapple, egg, and 1/3 cup butter to mix. Spread into a 8 x 8 pan. Blend cocnut, brown sugar and the remaining amount of butter. Sprinkle over batter. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.
Double and put in a 9 x 13 pan if doing for whole family.
