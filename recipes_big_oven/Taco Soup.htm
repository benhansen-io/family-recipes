Yields: 0


Ingredients

1/2 pound Brown Hamburger with diced onion
1 can Black beans washed and dried
1 can Pinto beans washed and dried
1 can Kidney beans washed and dried
1 can Original Rotel Tomatoes with Green Chilis
1 can Diced or stewed tomatoes
1 can Green Chilis
1/2 bag Frozen corn (or 1 can whole corn)
1 packet Taco Seasoning
1 packet Ranch Seasoning


 
Instructions
 Add all ingredients together and simmer 10-15 minutes.

Serve with sour cream and pepper jack cheese.

Cuisine :   Soup    

Main Ingredient :   Hamburger
