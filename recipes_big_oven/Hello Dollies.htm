Hello Dollies   


A thanksgiving Eve Hansentradition!
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1 stick Butter
1 1/2 cups Graham crackers crumbs
1 cup Walnuts coarsely chopped
6 ounce Chocolate chips semi sweet
1 1/3 cups Coconut flaked
1 1/3 cups Condensed milk eagle brand\
 
Instructions
 Melt butter. Pour in bottom of 9 x 13 inch pan. Layer above ingrediens in order. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes. Cool before serving>

Cuisine :   Dessert    

Main Ingredient :   Chocolate
