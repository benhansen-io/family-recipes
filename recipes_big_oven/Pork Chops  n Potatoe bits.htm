Dad's favorite
 Yields: 6 Servings

Ingredients

6 Pork chops
Vegetable oil
Durkee seasoned salt
1 can Cream of celery soup
1/2 cup Milk
1/2 cup Sour cream
1/4 teaspoon Pepper
1 package Frozen obrien or hashbrown potatoes thawed
1 cup Cheddar cheese shredded
1 can Durkee's french fried onions


 
Instructions
 Brown pork chops in lightly greased skillet. Sprinkle with seasoned salt and set aside. Combine soup, milk, sour cream, pepper and 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt. Stir in potatoes, 1/2 cup cheese and 1/2 cup french fried onions. Spoon mixture into 9 x 13 inch baking dish. Arrange pork chops over potatoes, bake, covered at 350°F for 40 minutes. Top with remaining cheese and onions; bake covered 5 minutes longer.
