Yields: 24 cookies

Ingredients

1/2 cup Shortening
1/3 cup Sugar granulated
1 egg
1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
1 teaspoon Baking soda
3/4 cup Creamy peanut butter
1/3 cup Light brown sugar packed
2 tablespoons Milk
1 1/2 cups All purpose flour


 
Instructions
 Directions
1. Heat oven to 375F. Remove wrappers from chocolates

2. Beat shortening and peanut butter in large bowl until well blended. Add 1/3 cup granulated sugar and brown sugar; beat until fluffy. addd egg, milk and vanilla; beat well. Stir together flour, baking soda and salt; gradually beat into peanut butter mixture.

3. Shape dough into 1- inch balls. Rollin granulated sugar; place on ungreased cookie sheet.

4. Bake 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned. Immediately press a chocolate into center of each cookie; cookie will crack around edges. remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Cool completely.
