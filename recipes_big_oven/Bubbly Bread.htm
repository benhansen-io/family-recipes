Bubbly Bread   


A perfect bread to go with ronzoni or any other noodle dish.
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

6 Rhodes Texas Rolls thawed
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese
Salad supreme to sprinkle on top
 
Instructions
 Thaw rolls until warm and bubbly. Knead into a ball; roll into a 10" circle. Sprinkle bread board with parmesan cheese. Roll out dough on top of parmesan cheese. Place on 12" pizza pan that has been sprayed with non-stick cooking spray. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and salad supreme. Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until lightly browned. Serve warm or store up to two days.
