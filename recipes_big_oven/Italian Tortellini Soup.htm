Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

4 cans (15 oz. each) S&W Italian Recipe Tomatoes -- basil and oregano,
2 cans (15 oz. each) Swanson Beef Broth
2 tablespoons Basil leaves
1/2 teaspoon Pepper
3/4 teaspoon Oregano leaves
1 medium Zuchinni sliced
3/4 pound Frozen green beans
1 pound Ground Sausage Emigration Market - not hot - Smith's is fine
6 ounces Frozen cheese tortellini costco - makes 2 recipies - freeze 1 portion if not used
Grated parmesan cheese for top
 
Instructions
 Combine first 7 ingredients in a large pot. Fry sausage and drain well. Add sausage and tomato broth and simmer about 2 hours for flavors to blend. 15 - 20 imutes before serving, add frozen tortellini. You may want to add a little water if you prefer a thinner consistency. Serve hot topped with parmesan cheese.
