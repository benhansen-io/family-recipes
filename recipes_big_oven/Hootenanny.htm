Hootenanny   


Great for Breakfeast or Dinner
 Yields: 2 Servings

Ingredients

1 cup Milk
1 cup Flour
1 teaspoon Salt
6 Eggs


 
Instructions
 1. Heat oven to 425 degrees.
2. Beat all ingredients in blender for 5 minutes.
3. Put 9 by 13 pan in oven with 1 cube of butter and let melt.
4. When melted put mixture into pan and bake exactly 20 minutes.

Serve immediatley with jam or syrup.
Delicious!
