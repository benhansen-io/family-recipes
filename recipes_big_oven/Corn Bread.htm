Ingredients

1 1/2 cup Flour
1 1/2 cup Cornmeal
1 3/4 cup Sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons Salt
1 tablespoon Baking powder
3/4 teaspoon Soda
3 Eggs
2 cup Buttermilk
1/2 cup Oil


 
Instructions
 Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients. Spray 9x13 pan with cooking spray and preheat in oven to 350°F. Pour in batter and bake for 30 minutes.
