Orzo Salad   


A unique and expensive, but addictive salad
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

Vinaigrette Portion:
1 1/4 cup Corn oil
1/2 cup Vinegar White wine
1 3/4 teaspoon Salt
1 3/4 teaspoon Pepper
1 tablespoon Garlic minced
1/2 teaspoon Basil dried
1/2 teaspoon Oregano
1/4 cup Parsley minced
Salad Portion:
6 cup Rice (optional) cooked
1 pound Orzo
3/4 pound Shrimp cooked peeled
2 cups Red or green pepper finely chopped
3/4 cup Red onions finely chopped
1 cup Artichoke hearts
1/4 cup Capers small
1/3 cup Parsley minced
1/3 cup Dill minced
1/2 cup Golden and dark raisins


 
Instructions
 Wisk the vinaigrette protion ingredients and set aside. Then make salad portion.
