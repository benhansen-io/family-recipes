Chicken Enchiladas   



Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

4 Chiken breasts cubed and boiled
2 cans Cream of chicken soup
1 can (12 oz) Green chili small
1 can Olives small can-sliced
2 cups Sour cream
1 bunch Green onion
2 cups Monterey jack cheese
1 dozen Flour tortillas 12 inch size
2 cups Cheddar cheese


 
Instructions
 mix ingredients except the last two. Put a little of the mixture (filing) on bottom of 9 X 13 pan. Put a side 2 cups of the filling.
Fill tortillas. Place in 9 X 13 pan and top with 2 cups of the filling and with the cheddar cheese.
BAKE @ 350 for 40-50 minutes
