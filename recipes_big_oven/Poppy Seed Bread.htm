s Poppy Seed Bread s   


Assemble: 20 minutes
Bake: 1 hour
 Yields: 2 Loaves

Ingredients

3 cups Flour sifted
2 1/2 cups Sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons Salt
1 1/2 teaspoons Baking powder
3 Eggs
1 1/2 cups Milk
1 1/8 cups Vegetable oil
1 1/2 teaspoons Poppy seeds
1 1/2 teaspoons Vanilla extract
1 1/2 teaspoons Almond extract
1 1/2 teaspoons Butter flavoring
Glaze:
3/4 cup Sugar
1/4 cup Orange juice
1/2 teaspoon Vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon Almond extract
1/2 teaspoon Butter flavoring


 
Instructions
 Grease and lightly flour two 9x5 inch loaf pans. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix all bread ingredients together, then beat for 2 minutes. Pour into pans and bake for 1 hour. Mix glazze ingredients together in a small bowl and pour over hot bread in pans. Let cool before removing from the pans.
