Italian Herb for Bread Machine   



Yields: 1 Bread

Ingredients

7 To 9 ounces Water
1 teaspoon Salt
1 1/2 teaspoon Vegetable oil
3 cups Bread flour
1/4 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 tablespoon Dried Parsley
2 teaspoon Sugar
2 teaspoon Dried onion flakes
1/2 teaspoon Basil
1/2 teaspoon Garlic powder
2 teaspoon Active Dry Yeast


 
Instructions
 Combine ingredients in this order. Use french bread setting.

Adapted from the Regal Bread Machine Manual.

Cuisine :   Bread    

Main Ingredient :   Flour
