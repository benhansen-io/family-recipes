Broken Glass Jello Salad   


A seely thanksgiving dinner tradition.
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1 small package Orange Jello
1 small package Cherry Jello
1 small package Lime Jello
1 small package Lemon Jello
1 cup Pineapple juice
1/4 cup Granulated white sugar
1 2 cup package Whipping cream
1 cup Graham crackers
1/4 cup Butter


 
Instructions
 Pour orange, cherry, and lime jellos into dish. Add 1 cup hot water and 1/2 cup cold water. Set in fridge and then cube jello. Mix lemon package with pineapple juice and sugar. Dissolve over heat. Add 1/2 cup cold water. Chill until syrupy. Combine syrupy jellow and whipping cream (stiff). Combine graham crackers with melted butter. Sprinkle to bottom of dish with the graham "crust".

Aunt Minnie Jo Seely
