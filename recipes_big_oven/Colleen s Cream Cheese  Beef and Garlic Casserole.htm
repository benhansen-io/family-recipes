Colleen's Cream Cheese, Beef and Garlic Casserole   



Yields: 7 Servings

Ingredients

8 ounces Ruffled egg noodles
1 cup Sour cream
3 ounces Cream cheese softened
6 Green onions medium
1 Garlic clove
1 pound Ground beef
15 ounces Tomato sauce with pieces can
1 teaspoon Salt
1 teaspoon Pepper
1 cup Kinds of cheese grated
 
Instructions
 Cook noodles; set aside.
Beat sour cream with cream cheese. Stir in green onions and garlic.
Cook meat and drain. Stir in tomato sauce, salt and pepper.
In oblong dish layer 1/2 noodles, 1/2 sour cream 1/2 beef then repeat layers.
Sprinkle with cheese.
Cover tight with plastic wrap; refrigerate overnight.
Cook the next day at 350°F for 30 minutes.

Cuisine :   Beef    

Main Ingredient :   Ground Beef
