Sunday Morning Rolls   


A great breakfast treat - if prepared the night before!
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1 cup Brown sugar
1 teaspoon Cinnamon
1 cup Butter
18 To 20 Rhoades Frozen Rolls


 
Instructions
 The night before, put sugar, butter, and cinnamon in a saucepan, bring to a boil and cook for 4 minutes. Place frozen rolls in a bundt pan, pour sugar mixture over rolls. Leave on kitchen counter overnight. In the morning, bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
