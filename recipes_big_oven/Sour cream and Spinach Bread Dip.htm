Sour cream and Spinach Bread Dip   



Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

2 cups Sour cream
2 cups Real mayo
1 package Lipton soup dry, country vegetable only
1 can Water chesnuts quartered
2 loaves French bread
1 package Frozen spinach chopped
 
Instructions
 Mix sour cream, soup packet, water, chestnuts and mayo. Put in refrigerator overnight. Defrost 1 package of chopped spinach in kitchen sink overnight. Next day, drain spinach and add to above mixture.
Break one loaf of french bread into bite size pieces. Hollow out middle of other loaf and place mixture inside hollow loaf. Refrigerate one more hour.

Cuisine :   Side Dish    

Main Ingredient :   Spinach
