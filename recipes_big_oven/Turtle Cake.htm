Turtle Cake   


A family favorite.
 Yields: 1 Pan

Ingredients

1 box German chocolate cake
1 cup Nuts chopped
16 ounce Carmels
12 ounce Evaporated milk
1/2 cup Butter melted
12 ounce Chocolate chips


 
Instructions
 Melt carmels with 1/4 cup evaporated milk in double boiler. Mix cake mix with 3/4 cup evaporated milk and melted butter. Pour 1/2 of batter into greased 9x13 pan. Bake 13 minutes in oven preheated to 350 degrees. Top with nuts, carmel mixture, and chocolate chips. Then add remaining evaporated milk to 2nd half of batter. Spread 2nd half of batter on top of cake. return cake to oven for at least 25 more minutes. Best if served warm with ice cream.
