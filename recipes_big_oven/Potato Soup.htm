Potato Soup   



Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1/4 cup Onions chopped
4 Potatoes medium
1/2 teaspoon Salt
8 ounces Cream cheese softened
3 cups Milk
4 tablespoons Butter
2 cubes Chicken Bouillion
1 1/2 cups Water
1/4 cup White Wheat Flour
1 cup Cheddar cheese


 
Instructions
 Saute onions in butter. Add potatoes, boullion, water and salt. Cover, simmer 15 minutes. Blend cheese, flour and milk until smooth. Stir into potatoes. Season to taste. Garnish with chives paprika, and mild cheddar cheese if desired.
