Chicken and Rice Crock Pot Dinner   



Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

4 chicken breasts
1 package Dry Italian Salad Dressing Mix
1/4 cup Water


 
Instructions
 cook in crock pot for 4 hours. (first hour on highm then reduce heat to low). After 4 hours of cooking, add 1 pkg of cream cheese and 1 can cream of chicken soup. Cook for 2 more hours. Serve over rice
