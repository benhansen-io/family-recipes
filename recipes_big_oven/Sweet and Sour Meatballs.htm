Sweet and Sour Meatballs   


Best meatball recipe yet.
 Yields: 0


Ingredients

Meetballs:
1 pound Ground beef
1/2 cup Oats
1 Egg slightly beaten
1/3 cup Onion
3/4 teaspoon Salt
3/4 teaspoon Pepper a few grains
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/3 cup Milk
Sauce:
1/2 cup Brown sugar
1/4 cup Barbecue sauce
1/4 cup Vinegar
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon Mustard


 
Instructions
 Combine meetball ingredients together and mix well. Form balls of 2' diameter. Place in casserole pan in single layer. Prepare sauce by blending together sauce ingredients (last 5) over heat until disolved. Pour sauce over meatballs.

Cuisine :   Beef    

Main Ingredient :   Ground Beef
