Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

3 medium Carrots cut into 1/4 inch pieces (1 1/2 cups)
1 large Green pepper cut into 1 inch pieces
1 medium Onion cut into wedges
2 tablespoons Quick-cooking tapioca
2 1/2 pounds Chicken pieces skinned and frozen
1 8 oz can Pineapple chunks juice pack
1/2 cup Packed brown sugar
1/2 cup Red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon Soy sauce
1/2 teaspoon Instant chicken boullion granules
1/4 teaspoon Garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon Ginger


 
Instructions
 -In cooker, combine carrots, green pepper and onion. Sprinkle tapioca over vegetables. Place frozen chicken pieces atop vegetables. For sauce, in a small bowl, combine undrained pineapple, brown sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, boullion granules, garlic powder, and ginger. Pour sauce over chicken pieces.
-Cover: Cook on low heat for 10-12 hours or on high heat setting for 5-6 hours.
