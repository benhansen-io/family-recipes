Banana Nut Bread Sticks   



Yields: 4 Sticks

Ingredients

1/2 cup Butter
1 cup Sugar
2 Eggs
2 large Bananas
2 cups Flour
1/8 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon Baking soda
1/2 cup Nuts chopped
 
Instructions
 Cream butter and sugar. Add 2 beaten eggs and stir well. Add mashed bananas. Sift flour, salt, and soda. Stir into mixture only until flour is mixed in. Add nuts. Fasten heavy duty foil to ends of bread stick pans and grease well. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 45 minuters. Makes 4 sticks.

Nancy Monson

Cuisine :   Bread
