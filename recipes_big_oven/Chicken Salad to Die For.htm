Here's a delicious salad that will serve a big crowed. It's one of Sherry Poulson's favorites.
 Yields: 20 Servings

Ingredients

1 12 oz pkg Bow tie pasta
1 12 oz pkg Colored spiral pasta
1 large can Pineapple tidbits drained
2 small cans Sliced water chesnuts drained and chopped
4 green onions chopped
4 whole Chicken breasts cooked and cubed
1 cup Grapes halved
6 ounces Craisins
1 cup Cashews coarsely chopped
1 16 oz bottle Kraft Coleslaw Dressing
1 cup Best Foods Mayonnaise


 
Instructions
 Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and cool. Combine mayonnaise and salad dressing. Place all other ingredients (including pastas), in a large bowl and lightly toss. Add dressing mixture and toss to coat.

If not serving immediatley cover and refrigerate. Add dressing and cashew just before serving.
Note: This salad is also good served with mixed green stirred in.
