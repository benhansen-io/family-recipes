s Ham and Patato Casserole s   



Yields: 0


Ingredients

8 Potatoes medium
1 cup Ham
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
1/4 cup Butter melted
1 cup Sour cream
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon Pepper
3 Green onions chopped
2 cups Kinds of chese grated
 
Instructions
 Boil 8 medium potatoes in their jackets till almost done in salted and baleaf water. When cool, grate and pour into buttered casserole dish. Add 1 cup chopped ham. Mix in seperate bowl: cream of chicken, butter, sour cream, salt and pepper, onions and 1 1/2 cups grated cheese. Pour mix over potatoes. Cover and bake at 350°F for 30-35 minutes. Sprinkle with 1/2 cups more cheese. Bake uncovered 10 minutes.
