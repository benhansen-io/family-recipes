Bean Delight   


Diane's Holiday Favorite
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1 can Baked beans
1 can Pork and Beans
3/4 cup Brown sugar
1 teaspoon Dry mustard
8 slices Bacon chopped
1/2 cup Ketchup


 
Instructions
 Empty one can in casserole pan. Sprinkle half of the sugar, mustard and bacon, then the other can of beans, then the rest of the ingredients. Bake at 325°F for 1 1/2 hours.

Cuisine :   Side Dish    

Main Ingredient :   Beans
