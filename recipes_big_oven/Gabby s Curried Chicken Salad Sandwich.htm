Gabby's Curried Chicken Salad Sandwich   



Yields: 4 Salads

Ingredients

2 1/2 cup Chicken cooked and diced
1 1/2 cup Green grapes halved
1 cup Celery thinly sliced
1/2 cup Peach chutney (major graze chutney-relish)
1/2 cup Coconut shredded and toasted
1/3 cup Bermuda (red) onion chopped fine
2 tablespoons Chives chopped fine
1/4 cup Sour cream
1/4 cup Mayonnaise
1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
1/2 teaspoon Madras curry powder
1/2 teaspoon Pepper ground
1/2 cup Almonds sliced
1 Cantaloupe
1 Honeydew Melon
Strawberries


 
Instructions
 Combine first 7 ingredients and toss.
In small bowl combine next 5 ingredients.
Pour the dressing mixture over the chicken and again toss lightly. Cover and chill. Place lettuce mound, chicken, sprinkle almonds and place fruit.

Cuisine :   Chicken    

Main Ingredient :   Chicken
