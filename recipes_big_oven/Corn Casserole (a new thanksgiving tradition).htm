s Corn Casserole (a new thanksgiving tradition) s   



Yields: 4 Servings

Ingredients

5 cups Frozen corn slightly thawed
2 cups Milk
2 Eggs well beaten
1/2 cup Sugar
2 cups Keeble Club Crackers crumbled
1/2 cup Butter


 
Instructions
 mix crumbled crackers and butter and set aside. mix corn and milk- simmer over low (John goes up to medium heat) Takes forever until simmering. Add well beaten eggs. Add sugar. Mix well.
Add 1/2 of cracker mix to full corn mix. Spread in 9X13" dish. Sprinkle 2nd half of cracker mix on top of dish. bake at 350 for 35 minutes

Paul Pennington
