Chicken and Dumplings with Veg - Low fat   



Yields: 6 Servings

Ingredients

2 tablespoons Margarine
1 pound halves Chicken breasts boneless skinless, cubed
1 1/2 cups Onions chopped
1 cup Celery chopped
3 cloves Garlic minced
1 pound Frozen mixed vegetables green giant
1 can Chicken stock ready to serve (14 1/2 oz)
3 tablespoons Parsley fresh chopped (or 3 t parsley flakes)
1 1/2 teaspooons Poultry seasoning
1 cup Skim milk
1/2 cup White wheat flour
1 can Buttermilk biscuits pillsbury, refrigerated
 
Instructions
 1) Melt margarine in a 4 quart saucepan over medium-high heat. Add chicken, onions, celery, and garlic: cook 12-15 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, stirring occasionally.

2) Add mixed vegetables, broth, parsley, and poultrey seasoning; mix well. Bring to a boil, stirring frequently.

3) Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup; In small bowl, combine milk and flour; blend until smooth. Add to chicken mixture; cook and stir until thicked and bubbly. Reduce heat to low.

4) Separate dough into 10 bisquits; cut each biscuit in half. Starting in center of sauce pan, arrange biscuit halves on top of chicken mixture in spiral formation. Cover tightly; cook 25-30 minutes or until biscuits are fluffy and no longer doughy.
