A family favorite since the early 1980's
 Yields: 0 Servings

Ingredients

1 package Cream cheese (8 onz.)
2 cups Chicken cooked and diced
1/4 cup Butter melted
1/8 teaspoon Pepper
4 ounce Canned mushrooms stems and pieces drained
3 cans Crescent rolls
1/2 cup Nuts chopped
2/3 cup Stuffing mix
1 can (12 oz) Cream of mushroom soup
1 cup Sour cream
serving Sauce
1 cup Sour cream
1 can Cream of Chicken Soup


 
Instructions
 combine butter and pepper.
fold in chicken and mushrooms
put 1 Tablespoon chicken mixture on rolls and roll up. seal ends
mult 1/3 cup butter and baste sides and top; roll in mixed nuts and stuffing (crush them together with rolling pin first)
BAKE 20 minutes @ 325

serve with a sauce (1 can cream cheese for 1 cup sour cream)
