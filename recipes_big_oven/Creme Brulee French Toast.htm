s Creme Brulee French Toast s   


An immediate family (and everyone's ) after Jordan Henderson's homecoming
 Yields: 12 Servings

Ingredients

1/2 cup Butter
1 cup Brown sugar packed
2 tablespoon Corn syrup
1 loaf Texas Toast Bread extra thick slices-or specialty bread
5 Eggs
1 1/2 cups Half-and-half cream
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1 teaspoon Vanilla
1 recipe Syrup butter see below
1 1/2 cups Butter
3 cups Maple Syrup


 
Instructions
 in a small saucepan melt butter, brown sugar, and corn syrup over medium heat until smooth, stirring occasionally. Pour mixture over onto large greased jelly roll pan ((18x13X1-inch). Spread to cover surface. Place 12 slices of toast bread in a single layer to cover pan. Mix together eggs, cream,salt, and vanilla. Spoon mixture over each piece of bread. Cover and refrigerate overnight. bake uncovered at 350 for about 30 minutes. Remove from pan and serve. If not serving immediately, turn each slice over(this will prevent sticking on the bottom of the pan) Serve with syrup butter.
Syrup butter: Melt syrup and butter together in sauce
